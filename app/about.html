Our Product!

We're glad you're using NUCoins!

The basic idea behind our product NUCoins is to introduce virtual money at NUST so the students can avoid carrying paper money all the time. So even if you lose your wallet, you won't lose any cash. With NUCoins transactions are now one click away. Tickets can now be bought and NUCoins can be transferred by a single click. NUCoins can be used to award students for their efforts whether that be in studies or any extra curricular activity. This will give them a very good incentive to work harder.
